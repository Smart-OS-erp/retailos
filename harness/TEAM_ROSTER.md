## Junior Minion call signs

Junior review or implementation tasks use Minionese-style call signs:

- Papoy — junior QA and test-fixture support
- Bello — junior frontend accessibility support
- Poopaye — junior data-quality support
- Tulaliloo — junior platform and documentation support

Juniors do not approve security, architecture, phase-gate, or production-release
decisions. The ranked owner remains accountable for every delegated result.
